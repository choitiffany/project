About the project

Racism, homophobia, sexism, classism: are just a few of the long list of forms of prejudice that people still experience today. Although we recognize the strides society has made in fighting for equality, it is undeniable that discrimination still exists. Everyday, people face prejudices and everyday, people lose themselves, their lives and their loved ones, to hateful words and actions. In lieu of the anger and pain we have witnessed these past years, we recognize the need to root activism for equality in love, rather than in anger, bitterness, sympathy or ignorance. "Story Share" seeks to generate a culture of compassion and love for people of different backgrounds and preferences. Through personal stories, we hope to educate and empower. We hope that both victims and bystanders will take this awareness and make a change in their community.
